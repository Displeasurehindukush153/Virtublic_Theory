## Statement

Critique without an institutional alternative becomes part of the system.

## Medium description

A blockchain critic selling courses about blockchain problems is not resistance. It is the absorption position: extracting capital from diagnosis without producing an alternative.

## Formal notation

```
Critique ∧ ¬Institutional_alternative → Absorption → Stability↑
```

## Detailed explanation

This mirrors the dynamic from Part I: platform critique is monetized and stabilizes the platform. The same applies to blockchain critique.

The difference between absorption and resistance is the presence of an institutional alternative. Virtublic is the attempt to move from diagnosis to construction.
