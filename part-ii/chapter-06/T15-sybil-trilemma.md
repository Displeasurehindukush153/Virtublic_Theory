## Statement

You cannot simultaneously have: decentralization + protection from fake accounts + no plutocracy.

## Medium description

To prove you are a unique human, you need either biometrics (centralization) or a large stake (plutocracy). There is no third option — without constitutional form.

## Formal notation

```
Decentralization ∧ Sybil_resistance ∧ ¬Plutocracy = ∅
```

## Detailed explanation

Worldcoin attempts to solve this via iris scanning — but this creates a massive centralized biometric database. Proof-of-stake solves via capital — but reproduces T11.

P13 (Civic Guard) resolves the trilemma through a constitutionally legitimate verification form: a randomly selected citizen panel, rotation, on-chain oath.
