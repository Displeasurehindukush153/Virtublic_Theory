## Statement

In a "1 token = 1 vote" system the wealthy automatically become wealthier and more influential.

## Medium description

Staking yields income proportional to capital. Capital grows. Influence grows with it. The Gini coefficient increases monotonically.

## Formal notation

```
Wealth(t+1) = Wealth(t)·(1+r)^t → Gini(t+1) ≥ Gini(t) always
```

## Detailed explanation

This reproduces T1 (surplus attention) on a new substrate: tokens instead of attention, staking instead of engagement. The mathematics is identical.

The ideology of "decentralization" does not change this mathematics. Blockchain without constitutional architecture reproduces plutocracy in a new package.
