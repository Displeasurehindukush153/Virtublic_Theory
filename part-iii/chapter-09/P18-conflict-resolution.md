## Statement

Any constitutional dispute is resolved through the objective structure of invariants, not through an arbitrator's choice.

## Formal notation

```
Res(P18) = a₋₁; conflict(Pi,Pj) → resolve(via P0 hierarchy)
```

## Detailed description

The outcome of any constitutional dispute is determined by the P0–P18 hierarchy, not by a personal decision of a judge or majority. P18 is the only invariant that survives any circumvention of the system.

This is analogous to the residue in complex analysis: whatever path a dispute takes through the system, the result is determined only by violations of P0–P7.
