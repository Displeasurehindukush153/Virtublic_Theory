## Statement

A randomly selected panel of citizen auditors for resolving disputed questions.

## Formal notation

```
VRF_select(21-99) ∧ 2/3_majority ∧ on-chain_oath
```

## Detailed description

21–99 citizens, selected via VRF (verifiable randomness), rotation, on-chain oath, decision by 2/3 qualified majority. This is the constitutionally legitimate means of verifying uniqueness (P3) without biometric centralization.

Dual Suspicion Protocol: stage 1 — automated behavioral tests; stage 2 — the panel. Resolves trilemma T15.
