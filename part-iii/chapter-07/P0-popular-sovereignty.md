## Statement

Power belongs to citizens — not token holders, not developers, not the state.

## Formal notation

```
∀ governance_decision: Legitimacy ← Citizens
```

## Detailed description

P0 is not merely a declaration. It is an algorithmic filter: every proposed change to the system is checked for compatibility with P0. Formal verification (Coq) automatically blocks incompatible proposals.

Three excluded claimants to power:
1. **Holders** — capital does not equal legitimacy (T11).
2. **Developers** — expertise does not equal mandate (T12).
3. **The state** — it is simultaneously a regulator and a buyer of predictions.
