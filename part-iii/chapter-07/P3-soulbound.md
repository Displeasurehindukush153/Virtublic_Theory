## Statement

You prove that you are a unique human without revealing who exactly.

## Formal notation

```
zk-proof(unique_human) ∧ ¬reveal(biographic_data)
```

## Detailed description

zk-SNARK (zero-knowledge proof): a mathematical proof that you are a real and unique human, without transmitting biographical data. The system knows: "this is a unique citizen". It does not know: name, age, address, history.

This is the resolution of trilemma T15: verification without centralization and without plutocracy.
