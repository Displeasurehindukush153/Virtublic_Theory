## Statement

Political equality is an absolute invariant. 1 citizen = 1 political vote.

## Formal notation

```
EQU⊥(c) = 1 ∀c ∈ Citizens
```

## Detailed description

EQU⊥ (the political dimension) is completely separated from VIC⊥ (the economic dimension). A wealthy citizen has one vote on political matters — the same as a poor one.

Conversion between EQU⊥ and VIC⊥ is constitutionally prohibited. Political influence cannot be "purchased" through economic contribution.
