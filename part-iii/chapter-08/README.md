# Emergency Procedures

_P8–P9: what happens when the system itself needs to change_

## Even the best constitution must have an update mechanism

Otherwise it becomes a prison. P8 and P9 are that mechanism, with the highest possible activation threshold.

## The Concordance Rule (75%/75%)

Activating P8 requires _simultaneously_: 75% in EQU⊥ (citizen votes) and 75% in VIC⊥ (contributor votes). Neither dimension can act without the other.

This is the structural answer to T8 (sovereignty rupture): the political and economic dimensions are separated — and both are required for legitimate change.

::formula
P8 activation:
  (turnout < 10% × 2 consecutive cycles)
  ∧ (legitimacy < 66% EQU⊥)
  ∧ (Concordance ≥ 75%/75%)
::

## P0 — absolute protection

Formal Verification automatically blocks any proposal within P8 that violates P0. The system cannot legitimately revoke its own source of legitimacy.
