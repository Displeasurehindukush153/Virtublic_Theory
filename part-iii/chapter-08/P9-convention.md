## Statement

The constitutional convention — the last resort for revising the entire constitutional architecture.

## Formal notation

```
Convention_dissolve: self-determined ∧ qualified_majority ∧ task_complete
```

## Detailed description

Convention is convened during a systemic crisis that cannot be resolved under P8. The dissolution of Convention is determined by Convention itself through qualified majority — after completing its task.

This is not a revolution. It is a constitutionally formalized mechanism for systemic renewal with clear conditions for activation and completion.
