## Statement

Protection of subjecthood cannot be secured by one person — a form external to the system is required.

## Medium description

This follows from T4–T6: individual effort is neutralized (T5), cognitive tools are eroded (T6), consequences are borne by the subject without power (T4).

## Formal notation

```
¬individual_protection → ∃ intersubjective_form with constitutional_status
```

## Detailed explanation

Intersubjective form is not simply "joining together". Any informal collective is absorbed by the system through the critique monetization mechanism. What is needed is a form that structurally exists _outside_ the logic of engagement.

This is the direct transition to T10 and Part III.
