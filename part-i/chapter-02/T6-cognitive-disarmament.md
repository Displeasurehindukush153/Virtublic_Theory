## Statement

Platforms erode precisely the capacities needed to recognize their harm.

## Medium description

Reflection, concentration, long-term thinking — these are what make you capable of critically evaluating the system. These are precisely the capacities that decline with chronic platform use.

## Formal notation

```
engagement_hours ↑ → cognitive_capacity ↓ → resistance ↓ → engagement ↑
```

## Detailed explanation

Research confirms: more than 3 hours of short-form video per day persistently reduces concentration capacity. This is not "addiction" in a weak sense — it is a physiological change.

The system creates cognitive debt: the more you use the platform, the harder it becomes to understand and exit it. This closed loop requires an external structure for resolution — the constitutional mechanism protecting offline time (P14).
