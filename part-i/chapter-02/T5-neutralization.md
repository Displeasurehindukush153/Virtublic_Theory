## Statement

Attempts at "digital detox" are neutralized by the system faster than the subject can adapt.

## Medium description

Ad blockers → platforms read them and adapt. Account deletion → the algorithm retains a shadow profile. Individual resistance always loses against a system specialized in overcoming it.

## Formal notation

```
adaptation_rate(system) > adaptation_rate(subject) structurally
```

## Detailed explanation

This is not pessimism — it is mathematics. The platform holds data from billions of resistance attempts. Every attempt you make is a training example for the system. You are always teaching it to be better against you.

Therefore the solution cannot be individual. This follows directly from T5 to T7.
