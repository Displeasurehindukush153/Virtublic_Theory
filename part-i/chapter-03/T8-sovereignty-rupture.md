## Statement

Your right to form beliefs independently and the platform's economic sovereignty are structurally incompatible.

## Medium description

The platform earns precisely by shaping your beliefs through its ranking algorithm. Your informational sovereignty and its revenue are direct competitors.

## Formal notation

```
informational_sovereignty(subject) ∩ economic_sovereignty(platform) = ∅
```

## Detailed explanation

The ranking algorithm is not a neutral filter. It shows you the reality that maximizes the next interaction. This shapes beliefs. Beliefs shape votes. Votes shape policy.

This is a direct contradiction of democracy. Constitutional separation (P4) is the only way to resolve it.
