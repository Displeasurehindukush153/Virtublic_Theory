## Statement

Every interaction produces value for the platform that exceeds what you receive in return.

## Medium description

This is not a system error — it is its structural norm. The gap between your compensation (convenience, entertainment) and the predictive value accumulated by the platform is the engine of digital capital growth.

## Formal notation

```
platform_value(τ) > subject_compensation(τ) for all τ
```

## Detailed explanation

When you watch a video, like a post, or simply scroll your feed — you are producing data. That data trains a model. The model predicts your next action. The prediction is sold to advertisers.

You receive content. The platform receives a **predictive profile**. The value gap is structural, not accidental. This is not the greed of individual managers: it is the mathematics of the system.

The only way to change this is to introduce an external constraint at the level of constitutional law.

P4 + P16 (dual sovereignty)
